Calgary Science Spelling Bee

Not your ordinary spelling bee - are you ready?

Why CSSC?

We are a student-led organization committed to provide a diverse and unique learning experience to junior high students in a setting that emphasizes both individuality and teamwork. Our mission is simple: bring students together to compete, to collaborate, and to spark deeper thinking about the connection of language to science.

What?

  • Science

    Learn diverse scientific vocabulary and concepts

  • Team (up to 3 people)

    Collaborate in groups of three for the ultimate prize

Format

  • INDIVIDUAL

    30 words, worth 1, 2, or 3 points. After hearing the word, competitors must write these correctly to win while in a time-constrained environment. Most points wins!

  • INDIVIDUAL

    Like a traditional spelling bee, competitors must orally spell words under a time limit. The last person standing wins.

  • TEAM

    After three preliminary cycles, the top eight teams will go head-to-head in one final round to determine the winner of the CSSC trophy

Awards

  • Gold medals Awarded to round winners
  • Silver medals Awarded to 2nd place round winners
  • Bronze medals Awarded to 3rd place round winners
  • Trophy For top team and school display
